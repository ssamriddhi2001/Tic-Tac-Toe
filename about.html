About the Game

Tic Tac Toe, also known as Noughts and Crosses, is a classic paper-and-pencil game that is easy to learn and can be played by people of all ages. The game is played on a 3x3 grid, and the objective is to get three of your symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

The game is played by two players, who take turns placing their symbol (usually X or O) in one of the empty spaces on the grid. The first player to get three of their symbols in a row wins the game. If all spaces on the grid are filled and no one has three in a row, the game is a tie.
